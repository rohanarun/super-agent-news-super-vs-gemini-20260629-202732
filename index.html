What Gemini is for — and where Super differs

Gemini

Gemini is Google’s general AI assistant and agent platform. In 2026, Google added computer-use capabilities to Gemini 3.5 Flash, letting it analyze screens and control parts of a computer for automation tasks.

Source: blog.google

Super

Super is a personal AI agent designed specifically to operate a computer the way a human would — clicking, typing, navigating — and to reuse past work through a computer-use cache.

That cache means repeated workflows don’t start from zero every time, making Super better and cheaper for ongoing computer-use automation.
